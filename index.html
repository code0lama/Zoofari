#Welcome To Zoofari

Why You Should Visit Zoofari Park?

Zoofari Park offers a captivating and enriching experience for visitors of all ages. From majestic lions to playful primates and graceful giraffes, the park is home to a diverse array of fascinating creatures from around the world. Unlike traditional zoos, Zoofari Park emphasizes conservation and education.Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a family looking for a fun day out, or someone seeking a peaceful retreat surrounded by nature, Zoofari Park promises an unforgettable adventure filled with wonder and discovery.

Our Visitors Feedback!

As a zoo veterinarian, Zoofari Park was a breath of fresh air. The drive-thru format allows visitors a unique glimpse into animal behavior, something we often struggle to replicate in traditional zoo settings. It's fantastic for both education and sparking visitor interest. The walk-through areas complement this well, offering interactive learning and enrichment activities. Most importantly, the spacious enclosures and focus on enrichment programs prioritize animal welfare, which is always a top concern for me. Zoofari Park strikes a great balance between entertainment and responsible animal care. I highly recommend it!

Janelle

The staff were friendly and eager to share their knowledge, adding depth to the experience. What stood out most was the zoo's edication to conservation and education, evident through interactive exhibits and informative signage. It was a delightful day spent learning about wildlife and supporting important conservation efforts.
